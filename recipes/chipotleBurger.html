Chipotle Chicken Burgers

Description:

These juicy, smoky and spicy chipotle chicken burgers are topped off with pepper Jack cheese, refreshing Pica de gallo, and creamy chipotle mayo. Only use 1 or 2 chipotle peppers if you like things a little milder. Use fresh store-made pica de gallo to save time.

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon sauce from chipotle peppers
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 1 pound ground chicken
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 1 tablespoon butter, melted

Steps:

  1. Stir mayonnaise, 1 tablespoon adobe sauce, lime juice, and the 1/8 teaspoon salt together in a small and bowl set aside.
  2. Combine chicken, carrots, peas, and celery in a saucepan.
  3. Preheat a large non-stick skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Heat oil in the same skillet over medium heat
  5. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until thick.
  6. Spread chipotle mayo on bottom buns and sprinkle with 1 tablespoon cilantro.
